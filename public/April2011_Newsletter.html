Samhita.org is a philanthropic initiative of the Nadathur Trust. Though samhita.org is meant for all stakeholders in the social sector, first phase of activity focuses on providing greater visibility, more resources and a support system for social organizations (with an initial focus on NGOs).

Entire portal has been developed on Ruby on Rails. Technical features highlighting the portal development are listed below:

Signup using Facebook: This feature helps individuals to register on portal using their facebook account.

TinyMCE editor: Integration of this feature makes life easy for all the different types of users on the portal in creation of "News & Articles" to be posted/published on site.

Thinking Sphinx: Inclusion of this library speeds up the search process on the portal. Furthermore the search has been filtered as per necessary criteria set,making the search more feasible.

Delayed Job: Implementation of this feature had helped in improving the performance of the system. It sends mails & notifications asynchronously.

Payment Gateway (CCAvenue): All the transactions that take place through donations to NGO's on the portal are being handled through the "CCAvenue" gateway.
