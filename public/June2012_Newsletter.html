OpenSource Contribution

puneruby.org has been launched as the Idea Hub for getting together Rubyists in Pune. Every Friday, we have a codathon at Josh Office where we work on ideas that people have proposed or are interested in. Follow @punerb and #opensourcefriday for more information on this. This has been an initiative of the Pune Rails Meetup.
