March 2012 Newsletter
Where Programming is an Art!

Hi,
This month has been a very interesting, successful and controversial month at Josh Software. So, we are sending out an unconventional news letter!

In the News

MeraDoctor.co - one of our clients was covered in the News. This is India's first Medical Help Line which is manned by a call centers comprising of qualified doctors. It caters to the rural and semi-rural population of India. We are in the process of revamping these systems to scale. Using Rails 3.2.2 and MongoDB for our Application and Asterisk and Adhearsion for the voice calling, this system would be one among the state of the art Rails applications!

Organizing RubyConf India

We were one of the Silver Sponsors and Gautam was one of the main organizers at RubyConf India. It was a largely successful event with about 400 participants and very well known key note speakers like Mikel Lindsaar (@lindsaar) and Charles Nutter (@headius).
Sandip (@sandipransing) and Shailesh (@patilshailesh) spoke on "VoIP on Rails in India" where they showcased Asterisk, Adhearsion and also demonstrated call bridging!
Gautam (@gautamrege) spoke on "Push it, push it, some more" which was about nodejs, Websockets and socket.io and setup a virtual dancefloor for people to join in and dance!
Gautam and Siva (@sivagollapalli) also presented Lightening Talks - 5 minutes talk which got really popular at the conference!.
It goes without saying that at the post conference dinners, we all had our share of fun!

OpenSource Contribution

ISPUnity is a gem that helps multiplex the capacity of multiple heterogeneous (broadband, wifi, lease line, data-card etc) connections from multiple internet providers. (https://github.com/aruntomar/isp_unity)

FeatherCMS is our effort to build a DIY feather-light CMS - no frills and thrills. Its still in the nascent stages. (https://github.com/joshsoftware/feather_cms)

OpenSource Support

We are the early backers of Yahuda Katz initiative on Kickstarter to build Rails.app (http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1397300529/railsapp) Amidst lot of controversy about GPL and paid open-source, we strongly support Yahuda in this endeavor - we plan to contribute via funds and also code with him!
