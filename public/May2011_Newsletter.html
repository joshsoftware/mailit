RedDotRubyConf, Singapore
The first Ruby Conference in South-East Asia was held in Singapore. Gautam presented on "Rails and Outsourcing" there. He has written about his experience in this post. The conference had prominent speakers from across the globe like Dave Thomas, Tom Preston Werner, Gregg Pollack and even Matz!

Geolocation Rails and MongoDb - A recipe for success
Geolocation and SoLoMo (Social Networking + Location Awareness + Mobile) seem to be part of the next technology wave. This post, written as an cooking recipe, details how Rails works seamlessly with MongoDB 2D geolocation indexing and integrates with Google Map APIs.

How to setup Scaffolding with Rails 3 using JQuery?
One of our Tutorial Initiatives - Rails scaffolding is a quick way to auto-generate modules in an application. If you want to auto-create the models, views, and controllers for a new resource in a single operation, scaffolding is the tool for the job.

To know more about Ruby on Rails please visit blog.joshsoftware.com


33 Needs - The crowdsourced funding site, where socially-minded startups can raise initial seed funding from individual contributors on the Web. Social startups post their "needs" in terms of how much money they are looking to raise, what problems they are going to solve and how they are going to do it, along with a video to help spread the word virally. People can invest $10, $100, $1,000 or more, and in return instead of getting shares in the company, they get a promised percentage of revenues for a specified period of time like 5 percent of revenues for three years. This was featured on Mashable and TechCrunch .
