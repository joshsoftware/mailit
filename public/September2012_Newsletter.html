Celebrating 5th Anniversary !!
Where Programming is an Art!

*** We are 5 years old this September ***

Its been 5 years since we started - but it still feels like we only started yesterday. Its been an awesome roller-coaster ride for Josh Software, the lows always leading to new highs! Its no coincidence that the rebranded logo and website have been launched on the 5 year anniversary - this is a new start for us, where we plan to take the company to the next level.

They say that if you can survive the first 1000 days of your business, you are good to go but after 2000 days, you better know where you are going next ! Indeed its true.

Milestones

  • We have risen from 4 employees in 2007 to 29 employees currently

  • Our revenues have increased about 80% year-on-year consistently for the past 5 years

  • We have been regular sponsors of RubyConf India for the past 3 years

  • We have published a book - Ruby and MongoDB Web Development Guide

  • We are among the top 10 sponsors for Yahuda Katz's Rails.app

  • We organize and run the Open Source Fridays and Pune Rails meetup
